Annual Brochures

UNOPS annual brochures are available below, highlighting our results and the services we have provided for our partners’ peacebuilding, humanitarian and development operations.

Sustainability, Focus & Excellence

Slideshow

To view the slideshow along with captions and credits:

UNOPS provides project management, infrastructure and procurement services that increase the effectiveness and sustainability of our partners’ projects. Our new brochure, Sustainability, Focus & Excellence, provides details about how these services have supported a range of peacebuilding, humanitarian and development projects. Alongside our operational results the report highlights our emphasis on national capacity, our drive for excellence in everything we do and our support to projects that have the most positive, long-term impact possible.
