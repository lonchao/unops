Kilinochchi District

Sector(s): Education

Partner(s): Sri Lanka

This project is intended to provide urgent requirements in selected schools in Kilinochchi District in the Northern Region of Sri Lanka. The project sites are scattered across the Divisions of Karachchi, Kandawalai and Poonagary. The objectives of the project are to: A. Improve the educational environment of schools in Kilinochchi District. B. Construct school buildings and teacher’s quarters C. Provide inbuilt black board for each class room and furniture for activity rooms and teacher’s quarters. UNOPS is providing project management, infrastructure and procurement services for 12 schools and three teachers quarters. The project is funded by the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA).

Duration: 31 October 2011 to 22 May 2015
Total Funded Amount: US$ 4,900,000
Total Delivery: US$ 1,875,143
